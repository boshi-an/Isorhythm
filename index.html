About

The purpose of this website is to demonstrate how isorhythmic music is written. Composer can simply choose a sequence of Color (pitch) and Talea (rhythm), then repeate them until they come back to the beginning, which forms a structure of circles rotating together.

You can type in Color in the box on the left, and Talea using the buttons on the right. After inputing both Color and Talea, you can click "Start" button to listen to the isorhythmic music you created.

This project is inspired by course Understanding Music History 1: Critical Approaches to Art Music in Europe, c800-1800 (2023-2024) at University of Edinburgh.
